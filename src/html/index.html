Virtual Room-P2PSP

Virtual Room
A virtual room where friends share videos among them in real time directly over the web browser, with synchronized playback and a video chat at the same time.
Make a virtual room


Stream Private Content

The video streamed remains anonymous as the peer to peer connection is built on browser-to-browser connection. This avoids passing the stream throgh server and maintaining the anonymity of the content.

Multiple user conferencing

Get started by clicking make a virtual room. Stream a video and video conference with multiple peers.

Anonymity of peers

User's anonymity is maintained by taking no information of the peer except the I.P. address and an alias(optional).

No plugins required

No external plugins/softwares are required to stream the content. Press the button and stream!
